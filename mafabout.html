    • We are a movement of women, men, allies, and partners who cares about the wellbeing of the girl-child and understands the importance of empowering the girl-child.

    We are creating a deliberate female-centered network of entrepreneurs, professionals in various career fields to contribute to the economic growth of our great nation.

    Our vision

    A world where the girl-child offers equal contribution to the economic growth and progress of our nation through proper education.

    A world where the girl-child is given equal status as the boy-child and are able to achieve their full potentialin all aspects of life.

    Our mission

    We know that we are more effective when we work together, and believe that - as a Partnership - we will foster real change in the life of the girl-child.

    Together, we will

    • Join forces to reduce the rate of abuse done on the girl-child and support girls who have been abused - or - is currently going through abuse.
    • Raise awareness of the ill done to the girl-child by abstening them from proper education by encouraging open, inclusive and informed discussion at the community, local and national level.
    • Facilitate learning aids and provide proper guidance to the girl-child as to how to go about getting a proper education.
    • Source, Mobilize all neccessary policies, financial and other support to encourage girl-child education

    Who we are

    Margaret Akanimoh Foundation is a humanitarian organisation situated in Nigeria commited to empowering the girl-child to reach their full potential through ensuring that the girl-child is previledged to proper education in any career path, free from hate and abuse.

    How we are Funded!

    Margaret Akanimoh Foundation gets it's funding through grants from governments, foundations and multilateral sources. Their generous contributions enables us to support the work of the Partnership and catalyse broader efforts to ensure the girl-child is empowered and educated.
